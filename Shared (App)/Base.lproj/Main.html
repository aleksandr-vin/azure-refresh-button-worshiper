How to Use

  1. Open Settings > Safari > Extensions.
  2. Tap Azure Refresh Button Worshiper and toggle Allow Extension.
  3. In the Permissions section, change it to Allow.
  4. Go to portal.azure.com.
  5. If you do not move your mouse for 10 seconds, Refresh button will be worshipped.
